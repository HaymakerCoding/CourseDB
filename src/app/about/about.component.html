CourseDB.golf

A new worldwide directory of golf courses!

There are 35,000 golf courses in the world. Our mission is to list as many golf courses as possible so when you are looking for information on courses in a specific region, you will have one site to check out and lead you to all the details you need. Soon we will open up “Course Reviews” so that you can see what others are saying and maybe even enter a review yourself.

We launched in August 2019 so it will take some time to build the database but feel free to use the quick load feature to enter a course name, website address, country and closest major city. As time permits we will contact golf course managers to assist them with fully populating and maintaining their course information on CourseDB.golf.

Feel free to contact us at any time for more information.

Course Managers!

List your course

Use quick load to get started with basic information then use “Create a Free Course Listing” to create an account with us and get access to our Course Admin tools.

These tools will allow you to create a listing for one or more courses that you are currently responsible for. Once a listing is created, you upload your own text, photos, links and promo images then maintain your listing so golfers always have your latest information.

Contact us to help you optimize your free listing so it is the best representation of your course possible!
